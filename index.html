ABOUT US

SHRI VENKATESHWARA ENGINEERING WORKS, manufacturers of lifts and, based at Hyderabad are the market leaders in the Lift industry and they have multi factories and operations across pan India and Overseas. This leading and most popular brand leads the market as no.1 in sales turn over, over a decade against all renowned MNC’S. SHRI VENKATESHWARA ENGINEERING WORKS is truly MADE IN INDIA Company established in 2021,and has been growing consistently year on year. Lift industry has been constantly growing with the urbanization and the consistent growth and expansion in tier 1 & 2 cities creating opportunities for housing, malls & commercial complexes. Along with it the growth in the public infrastructure systems like the modernization of airports, railway stations, metro line connectivities and foot over bridges in junctions has been pushing the industry to expand their manufacturing facilities and moreover created opportunities for technical man power increasing year on year. Since the lifts are technical products needing high level of periodical service and maintenance, trained & skilled man power in sufficient quantity has been a yearly requirement. Keeping this requirement in mind and to generate regular skilled man power for the industry and SHRI VENKATESHWARA ENGINEERING WORKS being pioneer in this field with its social responsibility envisaged to develop a skill manpower pool through intensive engineering training by nurturing and molding students during the curriculum thereby build a career for individuals.
